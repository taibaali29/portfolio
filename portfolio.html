About

Rigorous thinking applied to decisions that shape organisations and markets

I'm interested in the decisions that quietly determine whether companies compound value or slowly destroy it.

Growing up in India, I became curious about why similar organisations operating in the same markets often produce completely different outcomes. That curiosity gradually turned into a way of thinking: break complex problems into first principles, structure the key questions, and test them with real data.

My academic training in life sciences taught me analytical discipline. My professional experience in strategy and analytics pushed that discipline into business problems — from identifying growth levers and pricing dynamics to building financial and operational models that help teams make clearer decisions.

Alongside my work, I regularly study industries and build independent strategic analyses — examining capital allocation in healthcare systems, portfolio strategy in technology companies, and profit pool shifts in financial services. For me, the most interesting problems are rarely about more data; they are about asking better questions.

Strategy consulting appeals to me because it sits exactly at that intersection — rigorous thinking applied to decisions that shape organisations and markets.

Over the long term, I want to help leaders navigate complex strategic choices where clarity, judgment, and evidence matter equally.
